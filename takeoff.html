What is EasyJet  Switzerland Virtual

We are a virtual airline and operate as a non-profit association; our members are flight simulator pilots. We aim to make simulating EasyJet as close as possible to their real ops.


Joining EasyJet Switzerland Virtual

Joining EasyJet Virtual is free of charge.

We welcome experienced AND unexperienced pilots from around the world as members of EasyJet Switzerland Virtual. To be accepted, you must submit an application and fulfill the following requirements:

  • You must be 16 years or older
  • You have a good level of flight simulation experience/knowledge (Equivalent to a VATSIM ATPL or IVAO CP/ATP pilot rating).*

* You don't need to have a ATPL or CP/ATP Rating to join. We offer a special Virtual Airline Cadet Programme which you can join.

* To join EasyJet Virtual, press the "JOIN EASYJET VIRTUAL" Button and send us an E-Mail.
